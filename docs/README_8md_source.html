6 ## Goals
7 The goals of the project are as follows:
8  - be unconstrained by hardware
9  - be suitable for an embedded environment (no classes or inheiritance).
10  - be non-blocking
11 
12 ## Description
13 This driver attempts to establish communication with a GSM/GPRS modem using standard AT commands.
14 When the device is identified use connect() to authenticate with GPRS and open() establish a TCP stream.
15 
16 Once a socket is open data can be sent or recieved using a standard read/write/available interface.
17 An internal ring buffer is used to receive data, and a pre-allocated pool of packets is used to queue commands to
18 sent to the device. This allows the driver to be completely non-blocking without relying on complicated callbacks.
